## Residual limitations

- The theory section still does not solve the full Markov-perfect equilibrium of the large stochastic state space; the regime map remains computational.
- The model still abstracts from production. Welfare losses therefore come from redistribution under concavity together with theft, contracting, guarding, and punishment costs.
- The main phase diagram is intentionally interpreted as a medium-run regime map. Patronage appears clearly in event-time paths and contracting results, but on the chosen enforcement-effectiveness plane it is more often a transitional corridor than the dominant late regime.
